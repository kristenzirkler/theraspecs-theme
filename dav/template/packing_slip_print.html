How can I adjust the fit of my TheraSpecs?

All TheraSpecs frames are highly adjustable. The metal frames can simply be bent to shape, while the plastic frames adjust best if warmed with a hair dryer first. Go slowly while adjusting: tiny changes can make huge differences! Contact us if you would like more details. Note: adjusting your TheraSpecs will not change our 60 day no-hassle return policy.

How do I clean and care for my TheraSpecs?

Lightly brush off any grit and then clean them with lens cleaner and a microfiber cloth. The bag they come in works well for this. When you aren’t wearing your TheraSpecs, protect them with their microfiber bag or a case. (Tip: lens cleaner can be bought at many stores or you can make your own. Fill a small spray bottle with 2/3 rubbing alcohol and 1/3 water and then shake to mix.)

When should I wear my TheraSpecs?

We’ve seen three common patterns. First, some people wear their TheraSpecs whenever they are awake because they find that light is almost always painful or a trigger. Second, some people wear TheraSpecs whenever they’ll be exposed to light they know causes them problems, such as fluorescents or bright sunlight. Finally, some use their TheraSpecs for relief during acute attacks.

How long does it take for TheraSpecs to help?

Most people can feel a difference as soon as they put on their TheraSpecs, although it can take several weeks to adjust. This is most commonly reported by customers who are used to wearing sunglasses indoors.
